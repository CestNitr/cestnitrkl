INITIATIVE

CIVINAR

CIVINAR is online webinar and talk session organised by CEST Club of NIT Rourkela in collaboration with ICE:UK NIT Rourkela Chapter hosted via YouTube Live.The motive of CIVINAR is to provide the civil engineering community with diversified platform to share and gain knowledge about certain trailblazing of current and future interests in the Civil Engineering by noteworthy personalities who have attained expertise in their respective domains.

INTERVIEW

We the CEST & ICE has interviewed our proud aluminis to get to know about the outer shell of civil engineering and to guide us into the right path. This alumini talks provided a ocean of knowledge about the challenges to be faced in civil engineering field and how to encounter them.

AASRAY

AASRAY is an initiative by the founder to explore the various civil engineering minds, it is the fruit of months of hard-work and sheer dedication by the members. This is an ultimate go-to destination to know about the latest trends in civil engineering. With various articles ranging from Research, tributes, interviews & abstracts, this is a magazine to look out for.

CIVIBLOG

Our club now has taken a big step and reached a mile stone and have been a guidance for many mates with a new innovation CIVIBLOG which has been successfully launched in 2020. CIVIBLOG is a mini civil database providing pinnacle amount of knowledge consistently regarding new aspects and developments in field of civil engineering and helping out the students with information collected across the globe.

CIVIPEDIA

Civipedia(the cure to your curiosity) is an initiative by CEST, NITR that include information and knowledge related to civil engineering innovations from all parts of the world. Civipedia is been circulated in the website and other media like facebook, Instagram, telegram and linkedin. Civipedia is both interesting and informative to those who are anxious towards the civil engineering or related fields.

INNOVISION

Innovision, NITR is the official technical fest of the institute. It is one of the largest techno-management fests of eastern India generally conducted in the first week of November every year. Innovision welcomes everyone to participate in the fest which include a variety of technical events, workshops, celebrity nights etc
